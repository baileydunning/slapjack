How To Play

After you add both players, click "Start Game". Either player can slap on Jacks, Doubles & Sandwiche. In the event of a 'bad slap', the player forfeits a card to the other player. When a player has no more cards in their hand, the leading player deals continuously until a Jack is played. The other player has one more opportunity slap a Jack to avoid losing the game.

Player One Controls

Press "P" to deal,
"J" to slap

Player Two Controls

Press "Q" to deal,
"F" to slap
